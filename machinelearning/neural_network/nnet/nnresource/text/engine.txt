Estimate two aspects of an engine from two measurements.

An estimator can be found by Fitting the inputs and targets

The data set consists of 1199 samples.

"engineInputs" is an 2x1199 matrix, whose rows are:

1: Fuel rate
2: Speed

"engineTargets" is a 2x1199 matrix, whose rows are:

1: Torque
2: Nitrous oxide emissions
