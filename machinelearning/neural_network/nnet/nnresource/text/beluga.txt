Estimate the nursing time of a beluga whale calf from the six-hour time period postpartem index.

An estimator can be found by Fitting the inputs and targets

The data set consists of 223 samples.

"belugaInputs" is a 1x223 matrix of six-hour time period postpartem indices.

"belugaTargets" is a 1x223 matrix of nursing times in seconds.

----------

This data is available from:

http://pages.stern.nyu.edu/~jsimonof/Casebook/

S. Chatterjee, M. S. Handcock & J. S. Simonoff (1995). A Casebook for a First Course in Statistics and Data Analysis. Wiley, New York.
