Classify patients referred to a clinic as normal (not hypothyroid), hyperfunction or subnormal functioning, based on 21 patient measurements.

Samples may be classified using clustering (using only input data) or with Pattern Recognition or Fitting (with input and target data).

The data set consists of 7200 samples.

"thyroidInputs" is an 21x7200 matrix, whose rows are consist of 15 binary and 6 continuous patient attributes.

"thyroidTargets" is a 3x7200 matrix, where in each column indicates the correct category with a 1 in one element (and zeros in the two other elements).

1. Not hyperthyroid
2. Hyperfunction
3. Subnormal functioning
